# Glossary

**Agent**: The system acting under a mandate.

**AUMP**: Agentic User Mandate Protocol.

**Counterparty**: A merchant, seller, buyer, service provider, agent, or other
party the agent interacts with.

**Disclosure Policy**: Rules controlling what mandate-derived information may
be shared externally.

**Escalation**: Trusted review by the principal or an approved authority.

**Hard Constraint**: A requirement the agent must not violate.

**Mandate**: The representation contract between a principal and an agent.

**Principal**: The represented user, household, team, organization, or account.

**Protected Field**: Information the agent may use but must not disclose unless
policy allows it.

**Reservation Price**: A private negotiation boundary beyond which the agent
must walk away or escalate.

**Soft Preference**: A weighted or ranked desire that can be traded off within
mandate bounds.
